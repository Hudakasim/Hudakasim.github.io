Background

I'm a Software Engineering graduate from Istanbul Sabahattin Zaim University (GPA: 3.87, ranked 3rd in department and faculty), with a minor in Electrical & Electronics Engineering (GPA: 3.57). My work sits at the intersection of software and hardware — building autonomous systems that operate in the real world.
